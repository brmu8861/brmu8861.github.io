Thrillville

RollerCoaster Tycoon Lite

"You get to clean up puke and trash" -IGN

Thrillville had some of the most unique gameplay during the 2000s. From building coasters to running around and interacting with guests, it really had it all. Not to mention a very catchy soundtrack. It may be RollerCoaster Tycoon lite, but it is far more iconic.

You cannot make people fall off rollercoasters

This was a major vibe killer in the day

While you cannot put patrons on unfinnished RollerCoasters and send them to their doom, you can interact with them and flirt with them which is also pretty cool I guess.

5 Different Parks to Unlock!

Thrillville Timewarp, pictured to the left is one of five parks the player can unclock

While it only took a few hours to unlock all the parks, it made gamers feel really good about themselves.
