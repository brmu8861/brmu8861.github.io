Shrek 2: The Game

An iconic multiplayer masterpiece

"One of the greatest Shrek experiences of all time" - IGN

In this game based off the second installment of the iconic franchise, players enjoyed playing as all of their favorite Shrek characters. The game was very well recieved among 6-12 year olds who could play the game coorperatively with up to 4 players. While their were many fights over who got to be Donkey and Puss in Boots, the gameplay made for an enjoyable experience for all.

Unforgettable Boss Battles

It was truly iconic to battle iconic Shrek villians including a fight with Puss in Boots before he became a playable character. The battle involved the classic ps2 staple of mimicking the buttons Puss in Boots pressed increasingly quickly. And who could forget the final battle with the notorious Fairy Godmother? Not me.
